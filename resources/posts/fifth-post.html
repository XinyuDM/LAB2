iPhone 8's lifespan

The iPhone 8 was born on September 22, 2017 along with iOS 11. They were the sister phones of the iPhone 7, but with newer features. This smartphone was supported for nearly six years as its final release was iOS 16.7. With the release of iOS 17, the iPhone 8 remains unsupported encouraging new users to upgrade.
